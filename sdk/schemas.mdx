## Casino

- `CasinoStatus`: `online | offline | maintenance | suspended`
- `GameType`: common game identifiers
- `CasinoMetadata`: id, name, url, publicKey, status, metadata, timestamps
- `RegistrationRequest`: name, url, publicKey, signature, metadata
- `CasinoUpdateRequest`: partial update fields + signature
- `CasinoFilters`: status, games, limit, offset

## Heartbeat

- `HeartbeatPayload`: casinoId, status, timestamp, metrics, signature
- `HeartbeatResponse`: success, timestamp, nextHeartbeatIn?

## Protocol

- `FARE_PROTOCOL_VERSION`, `FARE_PROTOCOL_SPEC`
- `ApiResponse<T>` and `ApiResponseSchema(T)`
- `ErrorCodes` and `ErrorCode`
- `SdkConfig` and `SdkConfigSchema`
